Sport app

Events and informations from Football Federation ŚLZPN p. Rybnik

Description

Application is written for Silesian Football Federation 'ŚLZPN p. Rybnik'. App is a place where users can find football results, leagues tables, timetables from Silesian Football Federation ŚLZPN p. Rybnik. In a easilly way, through the dashboard, client can manages some part of the front part of the website. Also, they are able to post informations from the union's life. In the backend, application speaks with 'ekstranet PZPN', through the SOAP Client.
